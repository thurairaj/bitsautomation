About Us

Supercharge your business operations with BITS, the IT/OT convergence experts

At BITS, we recognize the significant role that power system and IoT automation play in modern business operations. As a leading provider of automation solutions, we are committed to delivering comprehensive, customized services that meet the unique needs of our clients. Our team of experienced automation experts leverages advanced technologies such as SCADA, PLCs, and DCS to enable real-time monitoring and control of critical infrastructure.

Our tailored automation solutions aim to optimize power system efficiency, productivity, and reliability while minimizing costs and reducing downtime. We offer a diverse range of services that include power system monitoring and control, fault detection and analysis, load shedding and load balancing, and voltage regulation. Through automation, we help our clients enhance the safety and security of their facilities, improve energy efficiency, and reduce their carbon footprint.

We remain committed to providing the highest level of service to our clients. We remain current with industry trends and emerging automation technologies to deliver innovative and sustainable solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients. Whether our clients operate in the energy, manufacturing, or transportation sectors, we are dedicated to providing them with the best power system automation solutions to enhance their business operations.
